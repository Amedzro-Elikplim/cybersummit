Cyber Security awareness Starts here...

The Cyber Security Summit, Ghana 2022 is a virtual program featuring experts in the field of cyber security, addressing hundreds of attendees in realtime. The aim is to create awareness among the general public on the how to secure the cyber space of Ghana and the threats that the lack of such security poses to Ghana as a country.

Discussion will be held on ways that an individual can protect himself when surfing on the internet and proven steps to start a career in Cybersecurity
